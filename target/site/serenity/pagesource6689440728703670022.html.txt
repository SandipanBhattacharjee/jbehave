<p><a href="#" title="That's what this widget is">Tooltips</a> can be attached to any element. When you hover<br />
the element with your mouse, the title attribute is displayed in a little box next to the element, just like a native tooltip.</p>
<p>But as it’s not a native tooltip, it can be styled. Any themes built with<br />
